Who We Are

A modern CA practice with traditional values.

CA Mohit Jain & Associates is a professionally managed Chartered Accountancy firm offering a comprehensive range of services in taxation, audit, accounting, and business advisory. Founded with the goal of delivering reliable, technology-enabled financial services, we serve individuals, startups, MSMEs, and established businesses.

Our approach is client-centric: we take the time to understand each client's situation and provide solutions that are practical, compliant, and aligned with long-term goals.

  • Member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI)
  • Cross-industry experience across manufacturing, services, and trading
  • End-to-end compliance and advisory under one roof
